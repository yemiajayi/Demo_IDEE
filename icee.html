President's Remark

"Welcome to IDEE 2014"

On behalf of the committee for the International Conference on Electrical Engineering 2014, I would like to welcome you for the upcoming conference which will be held from June 15 until June 19, 2014.

The IDEE 2014 will offer opportunities to share the most up-to-date scientific discoveries and innovative products. The ultimate objective is to advance our field of electrical engineering through networking among academia, industry, and affiliated specialists; and to build mutual understanding among colleagues from all over the world.

The scientific program will provide participants with opportunities to exchange the latest information, ideas, and experiences in the field of electrical engineering. Your important contributions of papers and presentations will make the event even more fruitful. Invaluable instructional lectures and special sessions will be prepared from outstanding electrical engineers.

Jeju is an island of peace with fabulous cultural and natural settings. It is the largest island in Korea and a famous tourist destination & recreational city of Northeast Asia. Because of its mild weather and abundant tour spots filled with beauty, it is known as the ‘Hawaii of Korea’. Numerous species of plants from polar to subtropical abound, making it a treasure trove of nearly 1,800 varieties.

I truly hope that you will take this chance to join us in Jeju, to benefit from this exciting technical program, and to plunge into natural beauty, mouthwatering food, and great weather.

We cordially invite you to join us and be part of this ICEE 2014. With your active participation and support, this conference will be a resounding success. I assure you that the event will be an academically enriching, socially enjoyable, and truly memorable experience for all delegates and guests.

Thank you very much.

Sincerely,

Mogli
Organization President, IDEE 2014
